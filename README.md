# HR Analysis Dashboard 📊

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing workforce demographics, promotion trends, and attrition patterns across an organization of 1,470 employees.

## 🎯 Project Objective
To consolidate employee demographics, promotion outlook, performance, and attrition data into a single interactive reporting tool — helping HR teams make data-driven decisions on workforce planning and retention.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** – Dashboard design & interactive visualizations
- **Excel/CSV** – Data cleaning and preparation
- **DAX** – Calculated measures and KPIs

## 📈 Dashboard Overview (3 Pages)

**Page 1 – Workforce Demographics**
- Total employees: 1,470 (882 Male, 588 Female)
- Department split: R&D (961), Sales (446), HR (63)
- Gender distribution by department

**Page 2 – Employee Profile & Promotion**
- Marital status: Married (673), Single (470), Divorced (327)
- Promotion anticipated: 72 employees (4.90%)
- Breakdown by job role, service years, job level, and commute distance

**Page 3 – Performance & Attrition**
- Total layoffs: 117 | Active employees: 1,353
- Promotion status by department
- Job satisfaction, performance ratings, and overtime trends (28.3% work overtime)
- Manager tenure analysis

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Workforce is 60% male-dominated, concentrated mainly in R&D
- Only 4.90% of employees are due for promotion, indicating limited upward mobility
- Attrition rate stands at ~8% of total headcount
- Research & Development shows the highest layoff and promotion activity, reflecting its larger workforce size

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Download `Human Resource Analysis.pbix`
2. Open in Power BI Desktop
3. Use the Navigator panel to filter by Gender, Department, Marital Status, Performance Rating, or Job Satisfaction
